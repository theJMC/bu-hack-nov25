# Lecturer Dash
Join 4 of your favorite lecturers as they dodge obstacles, leap through vines, and race across a continuously generating map!

Play with your friends and see who can last the longest in this one-screen multiplayer experience.

[Visit the Game Dashboard](https://dash.bedbugz.uk/)

## Features
- 'One Screen' View
- Multiplayer Functionality
- All your *favorite* lecturers
- Endless map generation
- Sound Effects!

## How to Play
Use gesture controls to control your lecturer’s actions:
- Jump — leap over spikes
- Clamber — climb through vines
- Slide — speed up when you’re falling behind

Avoid obstacles, keep up with the group, and outlast your colleagues! The last lecturer standing wins

[Join a hosted game on your phone](https://remote.dash.bedbugz.uk/)
